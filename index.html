About me

Hello! My name is Sayoko Wada (also known as Sadaeja Oh), a pianist originally from Osaka, Japan. I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Japan, followed by a Master’s degree at the Royal Welsh College of Music in the UK. Four years ago, I moved to Finland to pursue a second Master’s at the Sibelius Academy. I am now based in Finland, working as a pianist and piano teacher, sharing my passion for music through both performance and education.
